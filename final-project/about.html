About Me

I'm Christian Johnson

I am a Product Designer and Game Designer from Cincinnati, OH with a passion for gameplay systems,puzzles, and player interactions. I believe games are informative and transformative, and I always seek positive impacts on the players. My designs are experience-oriented, and I convey ideas through the player experience.

I am currently a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction student at Carnegie Mellon University. I received my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at North Carolina A&T State University. I previously worked as a design engineer at Microsoft and Amazon.
